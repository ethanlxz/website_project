FAQs

How to cancel or modify an order?

To cancel or modify an order, please follow these steps:

1. Log in to your account on our website.

2. Go to your order history or order details page.

3. Locate the order you wish to cancel or modify.

4. If the order is eligible for cancellation or modification, you will see options to do so. Follow the prompts to proceed.

5. If you encounter any difficulties or have questions, feel free to contact our customer service team for assistance.

What is our product warranty policy?

Our product warranty policy ensures that all products sold by us are free from defects in materials and workmanship under normal use for a specified period of time from the date of purchase. This warranty coverage varies depending on the type of product and may include repair, replacement, or refund options. For specific details about the warranty coverage for a particular product, please refer to the product documentation or contact our customer service team for assistance.

If unable to complete payment, what suggestions do we have?

If you're unable to complete payment, here are some suggestions:

1. Double-check the payment information you entered for accuracy.

2. Try using a different payment method if available.

3. Contact your bank or payment provider to ensure there are no issues with your account or payment method.

4. Clear your browser's cache and cookies, then try again.

5. If the problem persists, please contact our customer service team for further assistance.

How to process a refund?

To process a refund, please follow these steps:

1. Go to your order history or order details page.

2. Locate the order for which you would like a refund.

3. If the order is eligible for a refund, you will see an option to request a refund. Click on this option.

4. Follow the prompts to initiate the refund request and provide any necessary information.

5. Once your refund request is submitted, our team will review it and process the refund according to our refund policy.

6. Depending on the payment method used, it may take some time for the refunded amount to be credited back to your account.
