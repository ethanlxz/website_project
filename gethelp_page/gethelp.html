WHAT ARE NIKE'S PAYMENT OPTIONS?

We want to make buying your favorite Nike shoes and gear fast and easy, and accept the following payment options:

  • Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover
  • Apple Pay
  • PayPal
  • Klarna
  • QR Pay
  • Touch'n GO

Nike Members can store multiple credit or gift cards in their profile for faster checkout. If you're not already a Member, join us today.
